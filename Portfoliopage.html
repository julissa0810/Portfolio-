    Protest finder

    Protest finder was made to make the life of New yorkers easier by having a website/app that alerts them when a protest is going to happen. We also added a live feed, in case you are not able to attend a protest, you can still see the event, but going to these protest would also give a couple perks. For example: If you attend a certain amount of protest in your area, you will be given points and those points will eventually turn into some kind of money or coupon and they can possibly be used in Starbucks, Dunkin Donuts to name a few.
