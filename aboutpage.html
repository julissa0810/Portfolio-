"Who am I?"

My name is Julissa Juarez. I am 17 years od and a senior in high school. My parents were born and raised in Nicarauga while my twin brother and I were born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. Desrcibing myself is hard because I am so involved with people and I care about other people and forget to think about myself (in a good way). I asked a couple of close friends what comes to mind when they hear my name. They responded with butterflies, rainbows, rays of sunshine, genuine and happiness. I was surprised to hear their responses because I wouldn't have been able to describe myself in such a positive way, but these are things I want to keep around me because it is a part of who I and who I wil be.
